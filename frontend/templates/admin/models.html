AI Models & Technology Stack

Phase-1 Clinical Risk Prediction Model

Predicts patient mortality risk using structured clinical indicators.

  • Algorithm: Logistic Regression
  • Input: Lab + Comorbidity Features
  • Output: Risk Probability + Risk Tier
  • Purpose: Early Risk Stratification

Phase-2 Treatment Aware Outcome Model

Simulates outcome probability based on treatment scenario.

  • Algorithm: Logistic Regression (Treatment Conditional)
  • Input: Scaled Clinical Features + Treatment Flag
  • Output: Risk With / Without Antibiotic
  • Purpose: Clinical Decision Support
